Japanese Culture

Japan’s culture is a mix of ancient customs and modern ideas. People value respect, politeness, and community. Japanese culture includes art, anime, manga, music, technology, and traditions that go back thousands of years.

Many Japanese art styles, like origami and ink painting, have been practiced for centuries. At the same time, Japan is known worldwide for its modern culture like video games and animation.

The idea of respect is very important in Japanese culture — people bow when greeting and remove shoes before entering homes.
