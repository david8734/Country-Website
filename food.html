Food of Japan

Japanese food is known for being fresh, colorful, and beautifully prepared. Meals often include rice, noodles, vegetables, and seafood. Some dishes are eaten every day, while others are for special celebrations.

Sushi is one of the most famous Japanese dishes, made with rice and fresh fish. Ramen is another favorite — a warm noodle soup with many toppings.

Other popular foods include tempura, miso soup, rice bowls, and sweet desserts like mochi.
